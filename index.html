Who I am & what I do

I'm Abhishek, a computer science student based in New Delhi, India, passionate about Linux systems, open-source software, and building self-hosted infrastructure. I thrive in the terminal and believe in crafting tools that are both functional and elegant.

My daily driver is Arch Linux / CachyOS with Hyprland — a fully custom Wayland compositor setup I've spent months perfecting. I containerize everything with Docker, automate repetitive tasks with Bash, and run analytics stacks like Metabase & Apache Superset on self-hosted infrastructure.

Currently looking for internship opportunities in DevOps, systems, or backend engineering — remote-friendly or Delhi-based.
